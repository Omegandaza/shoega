Silver Tip Shave Brush by Baxter

Baxter

$90.00 

This product is currently sold out.

100% Natural Badger brush made with fine SILVER TIP hair, soft and durable. Badger hair has a great ability to retain water,which helps create a lather when mixed with a shave cream. This lather improves the barrier between the razor and skin, lifting facial hair to ensure a smoother shave with less irritation.

Usage

  • First, cleanse facial skin.
  • Rinse the brush in warm water, shake off excess water, and apply Super Close Shave Formula to the top of the badger brush.
  • Work the cream into beard, using oblique brush strokes.
  • For more lather, dip the tip of the brush in water and add more shave cream.
  • 100% Natural Badger brush
