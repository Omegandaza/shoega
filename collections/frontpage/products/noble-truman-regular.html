Truman Regular by Noble

Noble

$250.00 

This variant is currently sold out

Cincinnati's own Noble Denim prides themselves on not cutting corners.  Made in the USA and sourced with all American ingredients, Noble jeans are built with utility in mind.  They feature 14 oz red-line selvage from Cone Mills White Oak, lined back pockets for durability, American & Efird thread, copper buttons and rivets and a reclaimed leather / hand branded patch.

The Truman's are a regular, but modern fit.  Straight from the knee down, with a mid-rise and a little extra room in the thigh.  

They fit true to size in the waist and are pre-hemmed at a 34" length.
